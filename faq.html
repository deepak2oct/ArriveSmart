General Questions

What is ArriveSmart?

ArriveSmart is a web-based service designed to help travelers stay informed about their destination arrival. It uses real-time geolocation technology to alert users when they are nearing their specified destination.

How does ArriveSmart work?

ArriveSmart tracks your journey using your device's GPS coordinates. Once you set your destination, it monitors your proximity and notifies you when you are within 100 meters of your destination.

Is ArriveSmart free to use?

Yes, ArriveSmart is completely free to use. Simply access the website, set your destination, and allow the browser to use your location for the service to work effectively.

Usage and Compatibility

Do I need to download an app to use ArriveSmart?

No, ArriveSmart is a web-based service accessible directly through your browser. There's no need to download or install any applications.

Which browsers support ArriveSmart?

ArriveSmart works on most modern web browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Ensure your browser has location services enabled for optimal performance.

Privacy and Security

Is my location data secure with ArriveSmart?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. ArriveSmart uses your location data only for tracking your journey to provide timely alerts. Your data is not stored or shared with any third parties.

Can I disable location tracking after reaching my destination?

Yes, you can disable location tracking or close the ArriveSmart website once you've reached your destination. We do not continue tracking once you leave the site.

Troubleshooting

I'm not receiving alerts. What should I do?

Ensure your device's location services are enabled and that you've granted permission for ArriveSmart to use your location. Refresh the page if necessary or check your browser settings.
